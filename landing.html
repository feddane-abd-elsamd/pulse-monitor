FAQ

Common questions

How is Pulse different from HWiNFO or Task Manager? +
HWiNFO gives you depth (every sensor imaginable) with overwhelming UI. Task Manager gives you a quick glance. Pulse gives you decisions — AI-powered diagnosis that tells you what's actually happening and what to do. Plus mobile, alerts, and history.
Does the agent run in the background all the time? +
Yes, but it's designed to be invisible. The agent uses less than 0.5% CPU and around 80 MB RAM. It auto-starts with your OS and survives reboots. You won't notice it's there unless you open the dashboard.
What data do you send to the cloud? +
Just the metrics — CPU, RAM, temps, network speeds, etc. Never keystrokes, screen content, or file names. AI insights are sent to our inference API with the last hour of metrics only. You can self-host the AI model on Pro for full privacy.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. One click in settings, no email gymnastics. Your data stays accessible for 30 days after cancellation, then is permanently deleted. Annual subscribers get a prorated refund.
Is there a free trial of Pro? +
Yes — 14 days, no credit card required. You get all Pro features including AI insights and mobile. At the end you can subscribe or auto-revert to Free. We don't dark-pattern you into paying.
What platforms are supported? +
Agent: Windows 10/11, macOS 12+, Ubuntu 20.04+ (other distros via .deb/.rpm). Dashboard: any modern browser. Mobile: iOS 16+ and Android 12+.
